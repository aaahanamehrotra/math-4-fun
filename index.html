MAGICAL NINE

Multiplication of a number by nine or a series of nine in four simple steps !!! Read this article to know how

Steps to follow :-
  1. subtract 1 from the first multiplicant.
  2. write the extra 9s in the multiplier.
  3. subtract the result of step 1 from the remaining 9s in ghe multiplier.
  4. format the answer as | Result of Step 1 | Result of Step 2 | Result of Step 3 |
Example 1
46*999
  • Step 1 : Subtract 1 from 46. Hence, we get 45.
  • Step 2 : Write the extra 9s in the multiplier. 46 is a two digit number, and 999 is a three digit number. Therefore, there is one extra nine in the multiplier. Hence, we get 9.
  • Step 3 : subtract the result of step 1 from the remaining 9s in the multiplier. Therefore, we subtract 45 from 99. Hence, we get 54.
  • Step 4 : The answer is | Result of Step 1 | Result of Step 2 | Result of Step 3 |
    = 45954
